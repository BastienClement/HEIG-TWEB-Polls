How it works

1. Teacher starts a poll

At any moment, the teacher can decide to start a new poll from their list of predefined questions.

2. Students answer the poll

Students are invited to answer the poll with the answer they think is the correct one.

3. Get instant feedback

Get answers feedback in real-time. Both the teacher and the students can see answers statistics and check their own answer.
